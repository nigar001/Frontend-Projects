Albert James Moriarty

Earl Albert James Moriarty (アルバート・ジェームズ・モリアーティ, Arubāto Jēmuzu Moriāti) is one of the main characters of the MORIARTY THE PATRIOT series. He is the eldest of the Moriarty brothers, part of the identity that makes up the Lord of Crime. After serving in the army, he became the leader of the newly-founded MI6, though he laid down the position after choosing self-confinement in the Tower of London.
